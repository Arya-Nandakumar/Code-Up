ABOUT TINKERHUB

TinkerHub Foundation is a non-profit organisation that strives to nurture a new generation of highly skilled young people by utilising 21st-century technologies and learning methodologies. We cultivate a future breed of technically and socially capable young people.TinkerHub assists students with experiential learning in the areas of innovation and entrepreneurship. More specifically, we help students in learning as they progress and realise their own ideas and initiatives. Because in our opinion it is vital to ensure that young people have the appropriate social and technical abilities. The Foundationâ€™s core philosophy is to use the power of peer-to-peer mentoring and reciprocal learning from within college campuses and institutions to achieve these outcomes among people. Today TinkerHub provides a platform for anyone, be it a student or a technology enthusiast, who wishes to learn, tinker and experiment more with emerging technologies and technology skill sets. The basket of tech skill sets that TinkerHub focuses on, ranging from Machine Learning to Mobile App development; from Analytics to Augmented Reality is constantly evolving as per the emerging trends in technology, innovation and the needs of the new global technology ecosystem

ABOUT TINKERHUB WOMEN IN TECH

Tinkerhub Women in Tech is a wing of TinkerHub Community which is a space inside the community where women can easily connect with those women who have done outstanding work in the field of technology. They could freely address their challenges to those women who have already overcome these barriers and challenges and succeeded, so that women in embracing technology and bridging the gender gap. Tinkerhub Women in Tech has become much more active since may 2021 and together we started addressing the gender disparity in Tech and help our women learn, grow and have a comfortable space to share their thoughts and knowledge. Tinkerhub WIT is present on 60 campuses across Kerala, assisting over 600 students in achieving their goals.

ABOUT CODE-UP

Code-Up is an all Kerala women exclusive hackathon by TinkerHub Women in Tech that aims to empower tech enthusiast women in spite of their tech knowledge. It focuses on women who are eager to get into tech to build a strong foundational knowledge and also women who upskill their skills for a better portfolio. Code-Up is a free registration hackathon exclusive for women who are currently pursuing their degree, aims to bring women from different campuses across Kerala to learn, to build together and get inspired. Participants have to go through two rounds of selection procedure to participate in Code-Up. Constant support of mentors and guidelines will be provided for each team at every level of the process. Selected participant teams will be provided with workshops and bootcamp prior to the hackathon which help the team to approach the hackathon and build their product with much more confidence.
